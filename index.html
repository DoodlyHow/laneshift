LANE SHIFT

Experience the thrill of high-octane racing in Lane Shift — an immersive, interactive driving simulation like no other. You're placed in the driver's seat of a power-boosted supercar, equipped with a physical steering wheel, gear shifter, and functional pedals for acceleration and braking.

Designed for an interactive physical gameplay experience, Lane Shift challenges your reflexes and coordination as you weave through potholes and hazards at blazing speeds. Push the pedal to the metal to accelerate, slam the brakes to avoid damage, and use the shifter to instantly switch between forward and reverse driving.

With a dynamic environment and responsive vehicle feedback, Lane Shift delivers the ultimate race-ready simulation, making you feel every twist, turn, and impact as if you're really in the cockpit.
